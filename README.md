# Sales_AI
# 🚀 AI Sales Intelligence Agent

### Your AI Teammate for Smarter Sales & Revenue Growth

---

## 📌 Problem Statement

Sales teams waste time on manual prospecting, miss deal risks, and lose revenue due to lack of real-time insights.

---

## 💡 Solution

We built an **AI-powered Sales Intelligence Agent** that integrates with CRM-like data to:

* 🔍 Automate prospect research
* 📊 Detect deal risks in real-time
* 💬 Generate personalized outreach
* 💰 Recover revenue through smart interventions

👉 Not just a dashboard — an **AI teammate**.

---

## ✨ Key Features

### 🧠 AI Prospecting Agent

* Generates Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)
* Suggests high-quality prospects
* Writes personalized outreach emails

---

### 📉 Deal Intelligence Engine

* Tracks deal pipeline health
* Assigns **Risk Score (0–100)**
* Suggests **Next Best Action**

---

### 🔄 Revenue Recovery Agent

* Detects churn signals
* Recommends:

  * Follow-ups
  * Offers / discounts
  * Escalations

---

### ⚔️ Competitive Intelligence

* Analyze competitors
* Generate battle cards
* Provide winning pitch strategies

---

### 💬 AI Sales Assistant

* Chat-based interface
* Ask anything:

  * “Write a follow-up email”
  * “How to close this deal?”

---

## 🏗️ Tech Stack

* **Frontend:** Antigravity AI
* **UI:** Modern SaaS Dashboard (Dark/Light mode)
* **Data:** Mock CRM dataset
* **AI Simulation:** Prompt-driven logic

---

## 🧪 How It Works

1. User inputs prospect/company
2. AI generates insights & outreach
3. System monitors deal signals
4. AI flags risks + suggests actions
5. User interacts via AI Assistant

---

## 🎯 Why This Stands Out

* ⚡ Real-time decision support
* 🧩 Combines multiple agents in one platform
* 🧠 Explains AI decisions (transparency layer)
